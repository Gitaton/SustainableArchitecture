VEGETATION

Vegetation refers to the reintroduction of nature that was lost due to the construction of a structure.

THERMAL MASS

GREEN ROOFS

Applying a layer of plants or greenery to a surface of a structure(usually roofs). Provides passive cooling - Prevents solar radiation on materials. Provides insulation.

PASSIVE COOLING

Passive cooling is caused by shade caused by the plants, evaporation, and insulation.

PLANTS USED

A variety of plants can be used to help with sustainability.
When placing plants on facades to grow however, climbing plants are necessary.
